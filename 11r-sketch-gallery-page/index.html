Melting Chromium

A simulator depicting how a piece of chromium melts inspired by:
  • Experiments of The National University of Science and Technology's "Moscow Institute of Steels and Alloys" (MISiS)
  • Exhibits from the Museum of the Metallurgical Industry of PJSC Severstal

Melt the chromium by increasing the temperature using controller. Just drag it!

λ (melting point) = 1907℃
